    MeMAD is an EU funded H2020 research project (2018-2020) that delivered methods for efficient re-use and re-purposing of multilingual audiovisual content, with a particular emphasis on video management and digital storytelling.

  • Odeuropa Explorer

    source

    Odeuropa is an European research project which bundles expertise in sensory mining and olfactory heritage in order to develop novel methods to collect information about smell from (digital) text and image collections.
